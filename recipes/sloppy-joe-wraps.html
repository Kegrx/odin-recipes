Sloppy Joe Wraps

Recipe Description

A delicious and more clean way to enjoy sloppy joes.

This recipe takes advantage of tomato soup and salsa to create a new take on sloppy joes and uses tortilla wraps to make the meal less sloppy than the name suggests.

Ingredients

  • 1LB Ground Beef
  • Season Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 can Campbell's Tomato Soup
  • Half a jar of Tostito's Salsa
  • Tortilla Wraps
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Place ground beef in a frying pan and add Season Salt, Black Pepper, and Cayenne Pepper.
  2. Cook ground beef until brown.
  3. Add can of Tomato Soup and cook until soup is glazing to the bottom of the pan.
  4. Add salsa and cook until glazing to the bottom of the pan.
  5. Remove from heat and scrape glazing off the bottom of the pan.
  6. Place beef and cheese on tortilla and wrap.
