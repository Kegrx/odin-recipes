Ribeye Steak

Recipe Description

This recipe ensures a perfectly juicy ribeye steak every time.

Ribeye steak is packed with flavor without requiring any fancy ingredients, sauces, or marinades.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Garlic Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Bring steaks to room temperature.
  2. Preheat grill to 400 degrees.
  3. Season steaks liberally.
  4. Grill for 5 minutes per side.
  5. Dot the steaks with butter.
  6. Let the steaks rest for 5 to 10 minutes
