Grilled Ribeye Steak

Description

Following this recipe will produce a medium rare ribeye steak that is juicy, spicy, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Garlic Salt
  • Black Pepper, freshly ground
  • Onion Powder
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Allow steak to reach room temperature
  2. An hour before cooking, season both sides of the steak liberally with Garlic Salt, Black Pepper, Onion Powder, and Cayenne Pepper
  3. Heat grill to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  4. Cook for 5 minutes, then flip to other side and cook for 5 more minutes
  5. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes before serving
