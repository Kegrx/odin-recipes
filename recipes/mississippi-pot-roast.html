Mississippi Pot Roast

Recipe Description

The most delicious pot roast you will ever eat.

Made with just a few simple ingredients and slow cooked in the crockpot.

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Dry Ranch Mix Packet
  • Au Jus Gravy Packet
  • Butter
  • Pepperoncini Peppers
  • Mashed Potatoes

Steps

  1. Heat the Olive Oil in a frying pan.
  2. Season the Chuck Roast with Salt and Pepper.
  3. Sear both sides and the edges of the Chuck Roast until golden brown.
  4. Place the Chuck Roast in the Crock Pot.
  5. Sprinkle the Dry Ranch and Au Jus Packets over the Chuck Roast.
  6. Place the butter on the Chuck Roast.
  7. Place the Peppers on and around the Chuck Roast.
  8. Cook on low heat for 8 hours.
  9. Prepare the Mashed Potatoes separately in the meantime.
  10. Shred the cooked Chuck Roast with forks.
  11. Serve shredded Chuck Roast and Peppers over the Mashed Potatoes.
